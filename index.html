OnTimer

OnTimer is an iOS app that helps you never be late for a meeting by giving you simple, reliable alerts before your calendar events.

What the app does

OnTimer connects to your calendar so it can:

  • Read your upcoming events and meetings.
  • Send you a reminder before each meeting, using the alert time you choose in the app.
  • Let you control which calendars are included and how far in advance alerts should fire.

OnTimer does not function as a calendar editor. It is focused only on helping you show up on time.

How OnTimer uses your Google data

If you choose to connect a Google Account, OnTimer requests read-only access to your Google Calendar so it can:

  • Read your list of calendars.
  • Read event details such as title, time, location, and recurrence.

OnTimer does not create, edit, or delete events in your Google Calendar.

You can disconnect your Google Account from within the app at any time, which stops OnTimer from accessing your Google Calendar data going forward.

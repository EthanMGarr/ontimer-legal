Privacy Policy for OnTimer

Last Updated: November 14, 2025

In Short: OnTimer helps you never miss a meeting by creating smart alarms from your calendar events. We only access your calendar event times and titles to create alarms - we never read notes, attendees, or share your data with anyone.

1. Information We Collect

OnTimer accesses and processes the following information to provide our alarm services:

  • Calendar Event Data: We access event titles, start times, end times, and recurrence information from your Google Calendar to create timely alarms.
  • Email Address: We receive your email address through Google Sign-In for authentication purposes only.
  • App Preferences: We store your alarm preferences (timing, alert style, business hours settings) locally on your device.

2. What We DON'T Collect

Your privacy matters to us. OnTimer does NOT access, collect, or store:

  • Event notes or descriptions
  • Event locations
  • Meeting attendees or contacts
  • Email content
  • Any data from other apps
  • Your location or activity data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the calendar event information solely to:

  • Schedule smart alarms before your meetings
  • Display upcoming events in the app
  • Sync your calendar every 15 minutes to keep alarms up to date
  • Apply your alarm preferences (timing, filters, business hours)

That's it. We don't analyze your calendar for any other purpose, and we don't use your data for marketing, advertising, or analytics.

4. How We Store Your Data

OnTimer stores all data locally on your device only:

  • Calendar events are cached on your device for offline access
  • Your alarm preferences are stored in your device's secure storage
  • No calendar data is uploaded to our servers or any cloud service
  • Data is automatically removed when you delete the app

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, rent, or trade your data with anyone. Period.

The only external service that receives your data is:

  • Google: We use Google's Calendar API to read your events. Your data is transmitted securely directly between your device and Google's servers. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for details on how Google handles your data.

6. Premium Features & Subscriptions

OnTimer offers optional premium features through in-app purchases:

  • Payment Processing: All payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.
  • RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect basic subscription status information. Review RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Superwall: We use Superwall to display subscription offers. Review Superwall's Privacy Policy.

Premium features (multiple calendars, meeting end timers, etc.) are stored locally on your device.

7. Your Rights & Choices

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View your synced events anytime in the app
  • Disconnect: Disconnect your Google Calendar at any time from Settings
  • Delete: Deleting the app removes all locally stored data immediately
  • Revoke Permissions: Revoke calendar access through your device settings or Google account settings

8. Google Calendar API Disclosure

OnTimer's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically:

  • We only request the minimum calendar permissions needed (read-only access to events)
  • Calendar data is used solely for alarm creation functionality
  • We do not transfer calendar data to third parties (except as required for core functionality)
  • We do not use calendar data for serving advertisements
  • We do not allow humans to read calendar data unless necessary for security or compliance, with your explicit consent

9. Children's Privacy

OnTimer is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us immediately.

10. Data Security

We take security seriously:

  • All communication with Google's servers uses secure HTTPS encryption
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely using iOS Keychain
  • Local data is protected by iOS security features
  • We never log or store your authentication credentials

11. International Users

OnTimer is available worldwide. By using the app, you consent to your calendar event data being processed on your device and transmitted to Google's servers (which may be located in various countries). Google's data handling is subject to their privacy policy and international data transfer mechanisms.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new privacy policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Notifying you in-app for material changes

Your continued use of OnTimer after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated privacy policy.

13. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know whether your information is sold or shared (we don't sell or share your data)
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

14. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.
